## IDEAL CUSTOMER PROFILE

### Who

**Primary ICP:** Small Amazon seller, 1–5 people, €5K–€50K/month GMV, 10–200 products listed, selling real physical products (not dropshipping).

**They have:** A product catalog they want to list or re-list. No in-house copywriter or designer.

**Their pain:** 
- €50–200 for a freelancer per listing + €100–300 for a designer = €150–500 per product
- Or they do it themselves and the listing is mediocre → low conversions → they leave money on the table
- They know their listing is weak but don't have the budget or skills to fix it

**They think:** "If I could list 20 products properly instead of 5 mediocre ones, I'd make more money."

**Signs they need ProductPixl:**
- Selling on Amazon EU + Bol.com
- Have 10+ products to list or re-list
- Currently using freelancers, doing it manually, or using generic AI (ChatGPT for copy, basic Photoshop for images)
- Complaining about listing quality or cost in seller groups

### Secondary ICPs

1. **Amazon agencies** — manage 20–100+ client accounts, need scalable listing production
2. **DTC brands expanding to Amazon** — have the product, need to list fast across marketplaces
3. **Amazon consulting clients** — consultants who white-label or use the tool for client work

### Who is NOT a fit

- Enterprise sellers (too complex, different buying process)
- Dropshippers (no real product photo, no consistent brand)
- Social sellers / TikTok Shop sellers
- Sellers already paying €2K+/month for full agency retainer

## PRICING POSITIONING

Current pricing:
- Free: 10 credits (no card)
- Starter: €49.99/month → 150 credits = 50–150 listings
- Pro: €89.99/month → 500 credits = 166–500 listings
- Scale: €149.99/month → 1500 credits = 500–1500 listings
- Credit packs: €14.99/10, €69.99/50, €249.99/200

**The pricing problem:** Sellers don't think in "credits." They think in "listings."

**Rebrand the unit economics:**
- "From €0.50 per listing" (10 credits for €14.99 = €1.50/listing if Complete Package)
- "Less than a coffee per product listing"
- "Replaces a €200 freelancer invoice"

**The free tier is the weapon.** 10 free credits with no card required. Get them in, let the product convert them.

## MESSAGING FRAMEWORK

### Core Positioning

**"The only AI studio that generates your Amazon listing copy AND images from one photo — in 3 minutes, for €1.50."**

**Three pillars of the pitch:**
1. **Speed** — "3 minutes instead of 3 weeks"
2. **Cost** — "€1.50 instead of €200 for a copywriter + designer"
3. **Quality** — "Amazon-compliant copy and marketplace-native images, not generic AI output"

### Buyer-stage messaging

| Stage | Message |
|-------|---------|
| Cold awareness | "One photo. Complete Amazon listing. €1.50." |
| Researching | "The only tool that does copy + images in one workflow" |
| Considering | "Used by sellers in NL, DE, FR, UK, ES — 10 free credits on signup" |
| Ready to buy | "Start free. No card required. Generate your first listing in 3 minutes." |

### Objection responses

**"Why not just use ChatGPT?"**
→ "ChatGPT writes text. It doesn't know Amazon's 200-character title limit, bullet point density rules, or how to generate lifestyle A+ images. ProductPixl does all of it — and it's €1.50."

**"My freelancer is cheaper/better"**
→ "Maybe. But they're €200 per listing and 2 weeks of back-and-forth. We're €1.50 and 3 minutes. Use us for the first draft, your freelancer for refinement — or replace them entirely."

**"I've tried AI tools and the output is generic"**
→ "Generic AI doesn't know Amazon's algorithm. We trained on Amazon's content guidelines and marketplace conversion data. The copy is keyword-dense and marketplace-compliant, not generic blog content."

**"Is this allowed on Amazon?"**
→ "Yes. Our copy and images comply with Amazon EU content guidelines. We include a compliance check before every download."
