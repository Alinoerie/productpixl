## 1. PROJECT NAME & ELEVATOR PITCH

Name: productpixl
Casing: productpixl (lowercase in URL/brand, "ProductPixl" in marketing)

One-liner: Upload a product photo, get a complete Amazon or Bol.com listing
and A+ content images — copy and visuals, ready to publish in minutes.

The actual problem, stated plainly: A small seller listing a new product faces
4–6 hours of work before a single unit sells — keyword research, writing title
and bullet points, composing the description, designing infographic images, building
lifestyle A+ modules. They either hire a copywriter (€50–200) and a designer
(€100–300) per product, accept a weak generic listing that kills conversions,
or spend their own evenings on it. ProductPixl solves this by making a single
photo the entire input, and returning a fully-formed, marketplace-compliant
listing and image assets.

---

## 2. THE THREE STAKEHOLDERS

The end-user isn't just "the seller." Three actors interact with this product:

PRIMARY — The Seller
The person who uploads the photo and receives the output. Usually a small
team founder, operations manager, or ecommerce manager. Their pain: time, not
money. They'd rather spend €1 and 3 minutes than €300 and 4 hours. They have
creative taste but no design or copywriting skills.

SECONDARY — The Amazon/Bol.com Algorithm
Not a human — the marketplace itself. It rewards listings with: high keyword
density in title, all five bullet points filled, relevant backend keywords,
A+ content that increases dwell time, images that reduce returns. ProductPixl
must output that satisfies this system, not just look good to a human.

TERTIARY — The Seller's Customer
The person buying the product. A well-written listing with lifestyle imagery
reduces return rates and increases conversions. A+ content specifically
increases purchase confidence. ProductPixl ultimately serves the end buyer by
making the listing truthful, detailed, and visually rich.

---

## 3. END USER DETAIL

Target: Small-to-medium ecommerce sellers.
Team size: 1–5 people.
Marketplaces: Amazon EU (DE/NL/FR/IT/ES/UK) primary, Bol.com secondary.
Inventory type: Real physical products, not dropshipping.
Budget: Can afford €0.50–€2 per generation but not €300 per product.
Tech literacy: Comfortable with SaaS tools, uploads images, fills forms.
Device: Desktop-first (Amazon seller workflows are desktop-native).
Languages: English for V1; app UI does not need to be localized.
Excluded: Enterprise sellers (too complex), dropshippers (no real product photo),
social sellers (different workflow).

## 4. THE EXACT HAPPY PATH — STEP BY STEP

### Pre-signup
1. Seller Googles "how to list on Amazon faster" or "Amazon listing generator"

3. Sees: "One photo. Complete Amazon listing. €1.50 per generation."

   one click to see full output (no credits consumed)

### First Paid Generation (using free credits)
9. Seller uploads photo — JPG/PNG, minimum 1000x1000px, white or transparent
   background preferred but not required
10. AI immediately analyzes: detects product type (e.g. "crossbody bag"),
    dominant colors, material cues, style category, approximate price range
11. Modal asks: "Which marketplace?"
    - Amazon EU (DE) / Amazon EU (NL) / Amazon EU (FR) / Amazon EU (IT) /
      Amazon EU (ES) / Amazon UK / Bol.com / Other
12. Second modal: "What do you want?"
    - "Amazon Listing Only" / "A+ Content Images Only" / "Complete Package
      (Listing + A+)"
13. Third modal: "Add brand guidelines?" — optional, shows the brand form
    (see Section 5 below)
14. Seller confirms, credits deducted:
    - Listing Only: 1 credit
    - A+ Only: 2 credits
    - Complete Package: 3 credits
15. Generation begins — animated progress bar with live status messages:
    - "Analyzing your product photo..."
    - "Researching keywords for your category..."
    - "Writing your listing copy..."
    - "Generating lifestyle scene concepts..."
    - "Building your A+ infographic..."
16. Completion: 30–90 seconds total
17. Seller lands on Results page

### Results Page
18. Tabs: Copy | Images | Compliance
19. Copy tab shows: editable title, 5 bullet points, product description,
    backend keywords, suggested Amazon BSR categories
20. Images tab shows: generated hero image, lifestyle scene images (2–3),
    A+ infographic module (1 wide + 2 standard)
21. Compliance tab shows automated check:
    - Keyword density: ✓ Pass
    - Bullet length: ✓ Pass
    - Image dimensions: ✓ Pass
    - A+ module count: ⚠ Only 3 modules — Amazon recommends 5+
    - Banned content: ✓ No violations detected
22. Seller can:
    - Download individual assets
    - Download all as ZIP
    - Click "Regenerate this section" (costs 0.5 credits)
    - Click "Save to Brands" (if brand guidelines were set)
    - Click "Publish" → opens Amazon Seller Central / Bol.com Seller Portal
      in new tab (manual upload, not automated)
23. Credits deducted on confirmation, not on start

### Post-generation
24. Email sent: "Your ProductPixl generation is ready — [Product Name]"
25. On return visit: Dashboard shows all past generations with "Regenerate"
    and "Duplicate" actions
26. Seller clicks "Buy Credits" → chooses Starter / Pro / Scale → enters

### Subsequent Generations
27. Seller clicks "New Generation" from dashboard
28. Repeat steps 9–26
29. If brand guidelines saved: Step 13 auto-populates from their saved brand,
    with option to override for this generation only

## 5. THE MARKETPLACE COMPLIANCE ENGINE

This is the feature that makes ProductPixl useful across different marketplaces,
not just Amazon.

### Amazon EU Compliance Checks

| Check                    | Rule                                           | Output            |
|--------------------------|------------------------------------------------|-------------------|
| Title length             | Max 200 characters (Amazon EU)                 | Warning if >200   |
| Bullet point length      | Each bullet ≤ 500 characters                    | Warning if exceeded |
| Bullet count             | Must have 5 bullets                            | Warning if <5     |
| Keyword density          | Title has keywords in first 80 chars            | Pass/Warn/Fail   |
| Image resolution         | Min 1000x1000px, max 10000x10000px            | Pass/Warn/Fail   |
| Image background         | Main image pure white (#FFFFFF)                | Pass/Warn        |
| A+ module count          | 3–5 modules recommended                        | Warning if ≠5    |
| A+ text-to-image ratio   | ≤30% text in images                           | Pass/Warn        |
| Banned content           | No "best ever", "only", "money-back guarantee"| Flag if detected |
| Price representation     | No pre-downstrike pricing imagery              | Warning          |

### Bol.com Compliance Checks

| Check                    | Rule                                           |
|--------------------------|------------------------------------------------|
| Description format       | Plain text only, no HTML/Markdown              |
| Description length       | 500–5,000 characters                           |
| Product title            | ≤200 characters                                |
| Image specs              | Min 1000x1000px, JPG preferred                |
| Mandatory attributes     | EAN/barcode, condition, brand — flagged if missing |

### "Other" Marketplace
- User selects country/category manually
- Compliance checks disabled (per-platform ruleset would require separate work)
- Copy and images generated with marketplace-agnostic best practices
- Disclaimer shown: "Verify compliance with your marketplace's guidelines
  before publishing"

### How Marketplace Selection Affects Generation

Amazon selected:
- System prompt enforces Amazon content guidelines
- Copy structured to Amazon EU specifications (character limits per field)
- Keywords research primed for Amazon search algorithm
- A+ images generated to Amazon module dimensions

Bol.com selected:
- System prompt enforces Bol.com content rules
- Description generated as plain text (no HTML)
- Images generated at Bol.com specs
- EAN field prompted for before generation begins

Other selected:
- Generic best-practice generation
- No marketplace-specific optimization
- Disclaimer shown before download

## 8. OUTPUT FORMATS — FULL DETAIL

### Listing Copy Export

Formats: Plain text (.txt) or structured JSON

Fields included:
- product_name
- marketplace
- title (max 200 chars)
- bullet_1 through bullet_5 (max 500 chars each)
- description (plain text)
- backend_keywords (comma-separated)
- suggested_browse_nodes
- generation_timestamp
- compliance_score

File name: `[product_name]_listing.txt` or `[product_name]_listing.json`

### Image Export

Formats: PNG (recommended for Amazon), JPG (for Bol.com)

Resolutions generated per output:
- Hero/main image: 3000x3000px (Amazon main image spec)
- Secondary images: 1500x1500px (Amazon image carousel)
- A+ lifestyle wide: 3000x1500px (16:9)
- A+ standard: 1500x1500px (1:1)
- A+ wide infographic: 3000x2000px (3:2)

Image requirements met:
- sRGB color space (Amazon requirement)
- No EXIF metadata (Amazon strips this)
- All images named: `[product_name]_[image_type]_[number].png`

ZIP file name: `[product_name]_productpixl.zip`

### Compliance Report Export

Format: PDF
Contents: All compliance checks, pass/warn/fail status,
marketplace-specific guidance, timestamp
File name: `[product_name]_compliance.pdf`

### Full Generation Package (ZIP)

Contents:
- `[product_name]_listing.txt`
- `[product_name]_listing.json`
- All images (PNG/JPG as selected)
- `[product_name]_compliance.pdf`
- `[product_name]_brand.png` (logo, if brand guidelines set)

## 9. CREDIT SYSTEM — FULL MECHANICS

### Credit Deduction

- Deduction occurs on generation confirmation, NOT on upload
- If generation fails mid-way: credits NOT charged; seller can retry
- If generation partially succeeds (some outputs, some fail): credits
  charged only for what completed and was delivered
- Regeneration of a specific section: 0.5 credits
- A/B variant generation (Pro+): 1.5x base credit cost

### Credit Display

- Dashboard always shows: "You have X credits remaining"
- When credits < 10: yellow warning banner
- When credits = 0: block on generation, show upgrade prompt
- Credit usage history: table showing date, product name, credit cost,
  generation type

### Free Tier Specifics

- 10 credits: one-time on signup, no monthly refresh
- If account inactive 90+ days: credits frozen, not lost
  (reactivation unlocks them)
- Free users cannot buy individual credit packs — must upgrade to Starter+

## 10. ERROR STATES — EVERY CASE

### Image Upload Errors
- File too small (< 1000x1000px): "Your image is [actual size].
  Please upload at least 1000x1000px for best results."
- File too large (> 20MB): "File too large. Maximum size is 20MB."
- Unsupported format: "Please upload a JPG or PNG image."
- Upload interrupted: "Upload failed. Please try again."

### Generation Errors
- API timeout (> 2 minutes): "Generation timed out.
  Your credits have not been charged. Please try again."
- AI content filter triggered: "We couldn't complete this generation
  due to content policy. Please check your image and try again.
  Contact support if you believe this is an error."
- Partial failure (some outputs missing): Show what succeeded with
  warning: "Some outputs failed. 2 of 3 image types generated.
  Credits charged only for delivered outputs."

### Compliance Errors
- Bol.com selected but no EAN: Warning, not block:
  "No EAN detected. Add one in your product data before publishing to Bol.com."
- Amazon selected but image background not white:
  Warning: "Your main image background appears to not be pure white.
  Amazon requires a pure white background for the main product image.
  This may affect your listing quality score."

### Account / Billing Errors
- Card declined: "Payment failed. Please check your card details."
- Subscription upgrade failed: "We couldn't process your upgrade.
  No charges have been made. Please try again."
- Credit pack purchase failed: Same message as above.

## 11. CONTENT MODERATION

### Image Moderation
- All uploaded images pass through a content filter before generation starts
- Prohibited content that triggers block:
  - Weapons, explosives, military equipment
  - Counterfeit or replica goods
  - Adult content (sexual or explicit)
  - Illegal substances
  - Dangerous products (regulatory restricted items)
- If blocked: "We can't process this image. Please upload a different photo."
- No human review queue — immediate automated rejection

### Copy Moderation
- Generated copy passes through banned phrase filter
- Banned phrases detected: "best ever", "money-back guarantee",
  "certified organic" (unless brand guidelines explicitly claim it),
  "100% safe", "no side effects"
- If detected: that phrase is flagged in the compliance tab with a warning
- Not a block — still deliverable with warning

## 19. GENERATION AI — TECHNICAL APPROACH

### Image Analysis (Step 1)
- Model: GPT-4o with vision (via OpenAI or Replicate)
- Input: product photo URL
- Output: structured JSON with product_type, material, colors[],
  style_category, estimated_price_range, key_features[]

### Listing Copy Generation (Step 2)
- Model: GPT-4o (text)
- Inputs: analysis JSON + marketplace + brand guidelines
- Outputs: title, bullet_1–5, description, backend_keywords[],
  suggested_browse_nodes[]
- Prompt engineering: per-marketplace system prompt with compliance rules
  injected

### A+ Image Generation (Step 3)
- Model: Flux or Stable Diffusion (via Replicate)
- Inputs: product photo + brand colors + style + module type
- Output: 2–3 lifestyle scenes + 1–2 infographic layouts
- Each image type is a separate generation call
- Safety filter on outputs before delivery

### Generation Status Updates
- Real-time via polling or WebSocket (SSE) from backend
- Status states: queued, analyzing, generating_copy, generating_images,
  finalizing, complete, failed
- Progress percentage calculated from sub-step completion
