# ProductPixl GTM Sprint — 90 Days
**Sprint Start: May 28, 2026**
**Roles: Milo (GTM Strategist/Operator) + Ali (Decision Maker/Seller Voice)**

---

## HOW THIS SPRINT WORKS

Ali = the face. Milo = the engine.

Ali does the things only a human can do: posts in communities, records videos, makes personal outreach, makes judgment calls, gives product feedback.

Milo does everything else: research, content creation, scheduling, social posting, data analysis, reporting, outreach drafts, strategy refinement.

Both work daily. Ali estimates 1–2 hours/day. Milo runs continuously.

### Phase 1: PROOF OF CONCEPT (Days 1–30)

- Community presence established in NL Facebook groups
- First 10 blog posts published
- First 5 YouTube demo videos uploaded
- 5 beta users → 5 paying customers from personal network
- Conversion metrics baseline established
- 1–2 agency partnerships contacted

### Phase 2: CHANNEL VALIDATION (Days 31–60)
**Goal: 50 paying customers, 2 proven channels, paid ads tested**

- Scale what worked in Phase 1
- Google Ads running (€1,000/month test)
- First case study published
- Partnership pipeline: 5 agencies in conversation
- Content calendar running at full cadence
- G2/Capterra listing live with first reviews

### Phase 3: SCALE (Days 61–90)
**Goal: 100 paying customers, clear path to €10K MRR**

- Scale winning channels 3x
- Partnership deals closed (2–3 agencies)
- Shopify app listing live
- Referral loop started (existing customers refer new ones)
- Conversion optimization based on data
- Month 2 recap: what's working, what to kill

## DAILY SPRINT CADENCE

### Every Morning (Milo — 15 min)
1. Check signup/conversion data from yesterday
2. Review any community mentions or replies
3. Post in 1–2 relevant communities (scheduled posts or real-time)
4. Push 1 piece of content (blog, LinkedIn, or Twitter/X)
5. Send 3–5 personalized outreach messages

### Every Evening (Ali — 15 min)
1. Review Milo's daily report
2. Approve/modify tomorrow's content
3. Do 1 human task: reply to community thread, record a video, email a contact
4. Flag any product issues noticed during the day

### Every Friday (Both — 30 min)
1. Milo: Weekly data report (signups, conversions, content performance)
2. Ali: Review numbers, make strategic calls, plan next week
3. Both: What's working → double down. What's not → kill it.

### Every Monday (Both — 45 min)
1. Review previous week's metrics vs targets
2. Adjust sprint priorities
3. Ali approves week's content calendar
4. Milo's task list for the week finalized
